Twenty more proposals. No overlap. Circumflex in frame.

Set 02 explores territory the first round didn't touch — classical Roman inscription (Cinzel, Marcellus, Forum), display serifs with personality (Rozha, Ultra, Modak, Bagel Fat One), modern grotesques (Jost, Space Grotesk, Hanken), condensed editorial (Oswald, Fjalla), and a full monospace family for the operator-engineer territory (JetBrains, Major Mono, Space Mono). Same custom Ô across all twenty. Walk the room.
