Pick the mark.
Three rooms. Sixty doors.

HAUS OF SÔS needs a wordmark that earns its place. Boing did the job. It does not do the work. Below are sixty typographic directions arranged across three rooms. Each carries the custom Ô (the circumflex over the O in SÔS) as the brand's signature character. Walk the rooms. Send Sable the numbers you want to advance.

01

How to review

Don't overthink the first pass. Walk all three rooms, jot the numbers that stop you.

  1. Walk all three sets. Don't stop at one. Each room is a different typographic territory and the wordmark needs to defend itself across the full survey.
  2. Note the numbers that hold you. The mark that stops you on first read is usually the right answer. Trust the eye before the explanation.
  3. Send the shortlist back. Three to five numbers, ideally one from each set. Sable will scale them to hero, drop them into the splash, and we narrow.
